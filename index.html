About us

Canada Immigration Practices

Established 1998

Canada Immigration Practices (CIP) is a Canadian firm that offers a wide range of immigration and citizenship services. CIP is exclusive to Canadian Immigration law and assists clients in processing visas through Canadian consulates and embassies.

The firm's services include: obtaining visas for skilled workers, investors, entrepreneurs, and persons who are self-employed. The firm also provides business support and after-landing services for clients.

The founder: M. Jamil Azimzadeh

PhD, MBA

Dr. Azimzadeh is a member in good standing of the Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council (ICCRC). The ICCRC is a non-profit organization created to regulate immigration consultants. Proof of accreditation can be found on the ICCRC membership list by searching for RCIC # R407239.

Dr. Azimzadeh has acquired many years of experience working as a chartered accountant and as a consultant in areas and fields such as immigration, business, international trade, taxations and tribunals.

He has contributed to community activities and charitable services. He has worked as a volunteer for the national treasurer of AVI (a first aid provider), and as a volunter general auditor of COOPERATE, an association geared towards helping persons undergoing rehabilitation for substance abuse.
